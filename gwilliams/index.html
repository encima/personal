Who is this joker?

An excellent question. I am Christopher Gwilliams. Currently, I am a PhD student in the School of Computer Science and Informatics at Cardiff University. My research focusses on wireless sensor networks and the use of intelligent networks to determine the value of the data.
I code/have coded in a number of languages and I am a big fan of taking on projects (I just struggle to find the time to finish them all)!
